Damn Good Potatoes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Seriving Information

  • Servings - 2
  • Calories per Serving - 300

Ingredients

  • Fucking Potatoes
  • 1x Water

Directions

Take the potatoes and put them in the water. Boil the water for like a dozen minutes or so, stirring if you feel like it. Remove the water because you will just want to eat the potatoes and not the resulting potato water. Serve warm.
